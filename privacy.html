Privacy Policy for flush: make alarm beautiful

Last updated: November 13, 2025

flush studio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the mobile application flush: make alarm beautiful. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information if you decide to use our App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information, sensitive data, or user identifiers. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

2. Permissions Used

The app requests certain Android permissions strictly for core functionality:

  • INTERNET – used only for app stability, updates, or fetching non-personal data if needed (no user tracking).
  • USE_EXACT_ALARM & SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM – required to trigger reminders and alarms at the exact scheduled time.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – allows the app to show reminder and alarm notifications.
  • USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT – allows the alarm screen to appear on top of the lock screen when an alarm goes off.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – used to re-schedule alarms after the device restarts so you don’t miss reminders.

3. How the App Works

The app runs alarms locally on your device. All data (like alarm times and labels) stays stored only on your phone. Nothing is sent to our servers or shared with anyone.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud storage services. No personal or device data is transmitted outside your device.

5. Data Security

Since the app does not store or transmit personal data, there is no external risk of data leaks. All alarm data is kept locally on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
